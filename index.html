About

My Bio

Hi, I'm Sergio Soto (or just Soto if you'd like). I'm a recording artist, producer, and recording engineer from South Texas, currently based in New Orleans.

Right now, I am working towards my bachelor's degree in Hip Hop and RnB under my mentors U-P and Raney. I produce, write, and record music for myself and others spanning across multiple genres including house, RnB, Hip Hop, electronic, Funk, Neo-Soul, etc. My influences include Toro y Moi, KAYTRANADA, Tom Misch, Sade, Michael Jackson, and the list goes on. I am currently working on my own extended project while producing records for other artists as well.
